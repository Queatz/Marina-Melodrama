ABOUT THE MINI-MANSION

Located on tell-tale Chestnut St. in San Francisco's Marina, the Mini-Mansion serves as a communal go-to for creative development, and as a shared knowledgebase of success close to home, and in the global landscape.

In association with the members, their offers and their knowledge, and the parties organized at the Mini-Mansion, members have access to an invaluable mesh-network of creative know-how.
